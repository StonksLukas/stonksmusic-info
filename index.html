Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

This section describes how we handle your data. We believe in data minimalism and transparency.

1. Data Access and Usage

StonksMusic processes the following information through the Discord API:

  • User Identification: Your Discord User ID and Username.
  • Server Context: Server (Guild) IDs where the Bot is active.
  • Command Logs: Technical data (command type, server, timestamp) used exclusively for error diagnosis and policy violations.

2. Audio Data Disclaimer

StonksMusic does NOT record, store, or monitor any audio data from Discord voice channels. It only streams music from public sources to the channel.

3. Data Retention and Deletion (Self-Service)

Management of your data is handled through Discord's own system:

  • User Control: To delete your data, you must deauthorize the application in your Discord settings under User Settings > Authorized Apps.
  • Manual Deletion: As we do not store your profile in a separate permanent database, manual deletion by the developer is not possible. Deauthorization is the only way to revoke access.
